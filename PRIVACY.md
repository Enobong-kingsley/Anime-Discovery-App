# Privacy Policy for AnimeMuse

Last updated: (you can set today’s date)

AnimeMuse (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the AnimeMuse mobile application (“App”).

## 1. Information We Collect

AnimeMuse is designed primarily as an anime discovery and recommendation app. We aim to collect as little data as possible.

### a. Personal Information

We do not require users to create an account and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as:

* Name

* Email address

* Phone number

* Home address

### b. Non-Personal / Anonymous Information

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance and user experience, such as:

* App interactions (e.g. screens viewed)

* Device type and OS version

* Crash logs and performance metrics

* This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

## 2. How We Use Information

Any collected data is used strictly to:

* Improve app performance and stability

* Understand feature usage

* Fix bugs and crashes

* Enhance recommendation quality

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services

AnimeMuse may use trusted third-party services for analytics or crash reporting (such as Google Firebase).

These services may collect anonymous information in accordance with their own privacy policies:

* Google Analytics for Firebase

* Firebase Crashlytics

We encourage you to review Google’s privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Ads and Payments

AnimeMuse:

❌ Does not show third-party ads

❌ Does not process payments

❌ Does not include subscriptions or financial features

## 5. Children’s Privacy

AnimeMuse does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data processed by the app.
However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* Use AnimeMuse without providing personal information

* Request clarification about how data is handled

* Stop using the app at any time

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.
Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
